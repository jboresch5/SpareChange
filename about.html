About

Developed in 2019, SpareChange is the premier technology platform for fantasy bowling. By joining a league, bowling fans can interact and compete against each other by managing a team of PBA players throughout the season. Each PBA national tournament represents a fantasy matchup where league members compete against each other in an All Play or Total Points style format. Team owners must fill their lineup prior to each match up by choosing players from the tournament roster. However, each player has a corresponding salary and each team must stay below the salary cap set by the league commissioner. Players are awarded fantasy points through the SpareChange scoring system based on their performance throughout the tournament. Once the tournament has concluded, matchup winners will be determined by the total points scored by the team of bowlers.

Our Story

With no active fantasy bowling sites on the Internet, two college roommates merged their passions for bowling, statistics, and technology to draft a plan to bring bowling into the ever-growing world of fantasy sports. Once the PBA expanded their tournament schedule for the 2019 season and inked a multi-year television agreement with FOX Sports, there was no better time to turn the idea into a reality. While development began, the 2019 season was tracked manually with friends to gauge interest and test different rules and formats. In January 2019, the web application was built and ready to launch. The rest is history.
